Privacy Policy

ThreeDaily — Your privacy matters to us.

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

1. Overview

ThreeDaily ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, ThreeDaily.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Task Data: The tasks, notes, and reminders you create are stored locally on your device and synced via your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this data.
  • Feedback Information: If you contact us through the in-app feedback form, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message to respond to your inquiry.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We do not collect analytics, tracking data, or usage statistics.
  • Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers or system information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the ThreeDaily app functionality
  • To respond to your support requests and feedback
  • To improve our app based on your feedback

4. Data Storage and Security

Your task data is stored locally on your device and synced through Apple's iCloud infrastructure using CloudKit. This data is encrypted and protected by Apple's security measures. We do not have access to your task data stored in iCloud.

Feedback submissions are transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely on our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

ThreeDaily uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple iCloud/CloudKit: For data synchronization across your devices. Apple's Privacy Policy applies: apple.com/legal/privacy

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any data collection
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at iocompile67692@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

ThreeDaily is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain feedback submissions for up to 12 months after resolution, then delete them. Task data stored in iCloud remains under your control and is not retained by us.

9. Changes to This Policy
